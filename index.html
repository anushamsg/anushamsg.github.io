About

I'm a healthcare data analyst with a clinical background — a dentist who moved into health informatics to work with the data behind the care. I turn clinical and research data into evidence teams can act on, working in SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI across EHR, REDCap, and digital-health datasets.

My time chairside is what makes the analytics land: it tells me which questions are worth asking and keeps the patient in view of every dashboard and model. That same instinct makes me a strong researcher — I can run a rigorous, IRB-compliant study as readily as I can build a reporting pipeline, and I move between the two depending on what the problem needs.

Today I'm focused on healthcare-analytics roles where I can ship insights that measurably improve care. Longer term, I'm building toward management in the digital-health space — leading the products, teams, and strategy that decide whether technology truly helps clinicians and patients, not just measuring it after the fact. If you're working where care, data, and product meet, I'd love to connect.
